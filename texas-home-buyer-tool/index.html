Navigating the 2026 Texas Housing Market

Buying your first home in Texas requires a strategic approach to savings. With cities like Austin, Dallas, and Houston seeing rapid growth, the 2026 market demands a clear financial plan. This tool helps first-time buyers estimate the gap between their current savings and a standard 3.5% FHA or 5% conventional down payment, adjusted for Texas-specific property tax trends.

Texas State Programs: TSAHC and TDHCA

Texas offers incredible opportunities for first-time buyers through the Texas State Affordable Housing Corporation (TSAHC) and the Texas Department of Housing and Community Affairs (TDHCA). These programs often provide down payment assistance (DPA) that can cover up to 5% of your loan amount. In 2026, eligibility has been expanded to include more "homes for heroes" categories, including teachers and police officers across the state.
